Our
Story

Dropmark was born from a simple observation: branded water bottles at Indian corporate events were either overpriced or poorly made. Businesses were settling for generic bottles with cheap stickers slapped on them — and that was never good enough.

We set out to change that. By working directly with certified bottling plants and building an in-house design process, we created a pipeline that's fast, affordable, and genuinely premium. Every bottle that leaves our facility is something you'd be proud to hand to a client.

Today, we serve 500+ businesses across India — from boutique hotels in Jaipur to tech conferences in Bangalore, from wedding planners in Delhi to healthcare brands in Mumbai. The common thread: they all wanted their brand to feel polished, and we delivered.

We're not just a bottling company. We're a brand amplification platform that happens to work with water.
